About Me

and 3D modeling

finishing up this Fall 2015. My research involves programming MRIs and
developing algorithms to extract impactful information to guide interventional
cardiology procedures

When I'm away from my desk, I enjoy playing tennis, learning the art of poi and
exploring long-exposure photography.

Generic Drug Launch Prediction


Generic drugs make up approximately 80% of the entire drug market. That they are more profitable than their brand name counterparts make them attractive targets to determine whether predictions can be made about their launches.

Different aspects of generic drug releases have been modeled using the FDA database.

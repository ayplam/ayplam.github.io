Pulmonary Vein Isolation for Atrial Fibrillation


Atrial fibrillation (AFib) is the most prevalent sustained cardiac arrhythmia in which the atria quivers rapidly. Initiation of fibrillation has shown to be a result from triggers originating at the pulmonary veins. To prevent the triggers from reaching the atria, patients can undergo pulmonary vein isolation therapy
